About this Course

Most speakers start presentations by telling audiences their name and thanking audiences for their time. Introducing yourself is a boring way to start a presentation. If you want to strengthen your presentation skills, grab audiences' attention by starting your presentations in a powerful way. Most presentation skills training focuses on body language, uhs, ums and PowerPoint slides. If you really want to improve your presentation skills, focus instead on the structure of your presentations and how you assemble data. Powerful presentation skills start with an effective opening, followed by information that is put together in a compelling way. Strengthen your presentation skills by getting and keeping your audiences' attention by being different from other presenters. Shari Harley's presentation skills training focuses on the things that differentiate great speakers from boring, mediocre speakers.
