Our Values and Behaviours

Commitment

I am commited to providing solutions

Successful personal behaviours include:

  • Display a positive and genuine attitude with each other and our customers to improve outcomes.
  • Ask Questions to jointly define the problem and identify a quality of service solution.
  • Encourage new and better ways of doing things.

Loyalty

I am loyal to our company and our customers

Successful personal behaviours include:

  • Know who our customers are, understand their needs, and take their views into account.
  • Go the extra mile to deliver the best outcomes for each other and our customers.
  • Serve across all of our business units within our organisation courteously, fairly and effectively.

Excellence

I am competent and I strive for excellence

Successful personal behaviours include:

  • Model and uphold our code of conduct including displaying the highest standards of professional behaviour at all times.
  • Pursue professional growth and learning through our PDRs process and a commitment to improving development areas.
  • Value diversity, inclusion and commitment to safe and healthy work practices.

Can Do

I am possitive and I display a can do attitude

Successful personal behaviours include:

  • Choose a positive attitude as it always has a profound impact on the outcome.
  • Embrace setbacks and feedback without being discouraged and apply that knowledge to achieve better outcomes next time.
  • Listen attentively, question thoughtfully and discuss openly.

Caring

I am passionate and I am caring

Successful personal behaviours include:

  • Respect each other and be aware of your peer's personal situations; exhibiting empathy and professionalism towards their feelings.
  • Promote a fun, optimistic and communicative environment.
  • Acknowledge the contribution of our peers.
