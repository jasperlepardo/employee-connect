Subtle Bold Color Description Uses
Default Gray Use for a general status or state. To do, unavailable, minor, not started
Primary Blue For items that are in progress or that are current. In progress, open, modified
Success Green Use to celebrate a success. Available, done, approved, resolved, added
Info Violet Items in a new, created, or help state. New, information, help
Warning Yellow Items that require attention. Busy, blocked, missing, warning
Danger Yellow Indicates items that are problematic or removed. Errors, declined, failed, removed, critical
